Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Trakify ("we", "our", or "us") is a habit tracking and journaling mobile application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Trakify app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Email address or account identifier used for authentication.
  • User Content: Habits, journal entries (typed or voice-based), and progress-related data created by you.
  • Voice Input: When you use voice journaling, your speech is processed only to convert it into text. Audio recordings are not stored.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous and aggregated usage data such as feature interaction, screen views, and app performance.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data including device model, operating system version, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To provide habit tracking and journaling features
  • To authenticate users securely
  • To store and synchronize user data
  • To analyze app usage and improve functionality
  • To identify and fix technical issues

3. Analytics

Trakify uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This data is collected in an anonymized and aggregated manner and is used only to improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

4. Data Storage and Security

User data is stored using secure third-party services such as Firebase. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is shared only with trusted services that are necessary to operate the app (such as authentication, analytics, and cloud storage providers).

6. Permissions Used

Trakify may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Used only for converting speech to text during voice journaling.
  • Internet: Required for authentication, analytics, and data synchronization.

All permissions are used strictly to provide app functionality.

7. Data Deletion and User Control

You can delete your habits and journal entries directly from within the app at any time.

At present, Trakify does not provide an automated option to delete the user account. If you have questions or requests related to your account, you may contact us using the email address provided below.
